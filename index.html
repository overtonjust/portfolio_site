About me

I’m Justin, a fullstack software engineer. I spent the first 8 years of my career working in the culinary world of hospitality. And while I love the world of food, it was during my time as a hotel manager working alongside the company’s systems director where I got my first exposure to creating functions and what regex means in google sheets. That made me curious about how many other hidden functions and tools were just underneath the everyday tools I used in my day to day.

From there and a chat with my family I learned there was a program called Pursuit. There I realized there's much more to learn than just how to put a function in a simple spreadsheet. I invite you to follow me on my journey of web development built with React, Javascript/Typescript, express and Postgresql.

I'm excited to expand my expertise in cybersecurity through secure application development, with test driven development strengthening my defensive coding practices.
